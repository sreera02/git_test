Charama Slokas

Krishna Charama Sloka

Always think of Me, be devoted to Me, worship Me, and offer obeisance to Me. Doing so, you will certainly come to Me. This is My pledge to you, for you are very dear to Me. Abandon all varieties of dharmas and simply surrender unto Me alone. I shall liberate you from all sinful reactions; do not fear.

Varaha Charama Sloka

If anyone thinks of Me, when the mind is sound, with the body in good health and with all mental and physical faculties intact - if he thinks that I am indeed the very cause of this Universe or the form Vishwaroopam, that I am without birth - then I assure you, that I will remember this devotee of Mine, when he/she is on deathbed, lying like a stone or a log of wood, and personally take him/her to the Supreme Abode. - Varaha Purana

Rama Charama Sloka

He who seeks refuge in me just once, telling me that "I am yours" I shall give him assurance of safety against all types of beings. This is my solemn pledge. - Valmiki Ramayana 6.18.33

Sita Ma Charama Sloka

Kindness is to be shown by a noble person either towards a sinner or to a virtuous person or even to a person who deserves death, for, there is none who never commits a wrong. - Valmiki Ramayana 6.113.46
